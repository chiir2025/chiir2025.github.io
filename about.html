Travel Information

To learn more about travelling to and getting around Melbourne, check out Travel and Destination Info | Melbourne Convention Bureau.

How can I travel to Melbourne CBD from the airport?

You have a number of options to travel between Melbourne Airport and the CBD:

  • Ride share cars
  • Taxi
  • Car Rental
  • SkyBus Express Bus Service runs a regular express service from Melbourne Airport to the City Centre every 10 minutes, 24/7
  • A number of other bus services operate from the airport to areas throughout Victoria. Information on destinations and schedules can be obtained from information desks within the airport, or through the link below. These bus companies are authorised by Public Transport Victoria and Melbourne Airport to provide regular passenger services from the airport.

Things to do in Melbourne

Melbourne is a vibrant city with much to offer visitors. There are many great sites to visit that are close to the conference venue, including:

  • NGV International Art Gallery
  • Melbourne Zoo
  • Royal Botanic Gardens
  • Melbourne Museum
  • Old Melbourne Gaol

For a full list of Melbourne's attractions, visit See and Do, Melbourne, Victoria, Australia.

Melbourne has a thriving art and comedy scene. If you're looking to attend a show or live event, check out What's On in Melbourne.
